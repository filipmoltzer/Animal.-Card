Komodo Dragon

The worlds biggest lizard!

  • Scientific Name: Komodo
  • Average Length: 3 meter
  • Average Lifespan: 7 years
  • Habitat: Tropic

This lizard is found in the Indonesian islands of Komodo, Rinca, Flores, Gili Motang, and Padar. A member of the monitor lizard family Varanidae, it is the largest living species of lizard, growing to a general length of 3 metres (10 ft) in rare cases and weighing up to approximately 70 kilograms (150 lb).
